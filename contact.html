Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How quickly can I expect a response to my message?

We typically respond to all inquiries within 24 hours during business days (Monday-Friday). If you contact us over the weekend, we'll respond by the next business day.

Is the information I share confidential?

Yes, all information shared through our contact forms, emails, or phone calls is kept strictly confidential in accordance with our privacy policy. We adhere to HIPAA guidelines for all personal health information.

Do you provide emergency mental health services?

While we strive to respond quickly, we are not an emergency service. If you or someone you know is in immediate crisis, please call 911, go to your nearest emergency room, or contact a crisis hotline like the National Suicide Prevention Lifeline at 988.

Can I schedule a therapy session through this contact form?

You can request information about therapy services, but to schedule an appointment, we recommend visiting our Therapists page where you can directly book with one of our licensed professionals.
