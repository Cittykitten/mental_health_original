Coping Strategies

Techniques to help manage crisis moments

Grounding Techniques

  • 5-4-3-2-1 Method: Name 5 things you see, 4 things you feel, 3 things you hear, 2 things you smell, 1 thing you taste
  • Box Breathing: Inhale for 4 counts, hold for 4 counts, exhale for 4 counts, hold for 4 counts
  • Temperature Change: Hold an ice cube or splash cold water on your face

Safety Planning

  • Identify trusted people you can contact
  • List comforting activities or distractions
  • Note safe places you can go
  • Remove access to means of self-harm if possible
  • Have emergency numbers easily accessible

Self-Care in Crisis

  • Be gentle with yourself - this moment will pass
  • Use comforting sensory items (soft blanket, calming scent)
  • Listen to soothing music or sounds
  • Write down your thoughts to process them
  • Remember past moments when you got through difficult times
