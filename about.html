Our Journey

How Mindful came to be and where we're headed

2018 - The Beginning

Founded by a group of mental health advocates who saw the need for accessible resources in our community. Started as a small local support group meeting in coffee shops.

2020 - Going Digital

Launched our first website during the pandemic to provide virtual support. Reached 10,000 users in the first six months.

2022 - National Recognition

Featured in Mental Health America's annual report as an innovative approach to community mental health support.

2025 - The Mindful Platform

Launched our comprehensive digital platform connecting users with therapists, resources, and peer support nationwide.

Future - Global Reach

Our vision is to expand internationally, breaking down barriers to mental health care worldwide.

Our Mission

To make mental health support accessible, affordable, and stigma-free for everyone, regardless of background or circumstance.

Our Vision

A world where mental health is prioritized equally with physical health, and everyone has the tools they need to thrive.

Our Values

Compassion, Innovation, Integrity, Accessibility, and Community are at the core of everything we do.
